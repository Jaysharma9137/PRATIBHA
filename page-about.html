  • Fibre to Fashion: Where Innovation Meets Sustainability/ Creating Impact, From Fibre to Fashion

Pratibha Syntex has been a pioneer in the sustainable Fibre-to-Fashion (farm to fashion) industry. Beginning with a spinning facility of 24,000 spindles, we have steadily expanded our operations, incorporating Knitting, Dyeing, and Garmenting units by 2003. Guided by our purpose of spreading hope, joy, and abundance through responsible and responsive fashion, we are committed to transforming the textile industry through innovation, ethical practices, and a dedication to environmental stewardship.

Foreword from the Chairman

Here’s a more compact version of the message: Since 1997, Pratibha has evolved from a spinning facility into a global textile leader, driven by innovation, sustainability, and a dedicated team. Our vision is to create a meaningful impact—empowering farmers, fostering a culture of well-being, and delivering value beyond profits. Every milestone reflects our values of integrity, responsibility, and collaboration. Looking ahead, we are optimistic about setting new standards in sustainability and community empowerment. Thank you for being a key part of our journey.

Foreword from the Managing Director

At Pratibha Syntex, we are committed to sustainability, innovation, and community empowerment. From a small start, we've grown into a globally recognized textile organization. Our focus on empowering communities, leveraging technology, and adhering to ESG goals drives us to connect farmers, employees, and brands with a shared vision of sustainability. The future is bright, and together, we can lead the way in creating sustainable textiles. Thank you for your trust and support. Let’s shape the future together.

Founded in 1997, Pratibha Syntex is a vertically integrated textile manufacturer in Pithampur, Madhya Pradesh, near India’s organic cotton belt. Guided by the ethos "वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम्" (The Earth is one family), we focus on sustainable products, a happy workforce, eco-friendly processes, and the well-being of all.
